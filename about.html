#About Us

Welcome to berber carpet your ultimate destination for authentic Berber carpets. With a passion for craftsmanship and a commitment to preserving traditional weaving techniques, we bring you the finest selection of Berber carpets sourced directly from the artisans of North Africa. Each rug tells a story of centuries-old tradition, passed down through generations of skilled weavers. Our mission is to share the beauty and cultural significance of Berber carpets with the world, offering you not just a piece of decor, but a piece of history for your home. Explore our collection and experience the timeless elegance of Berber craftsmanship.

Moroccan Berber carpets: an ancestral art

Objects that are simply out of the ordinary. Thanks to a very particular know-how transmitted from generation to generation, the weavers imagine and weave daring carpets. We love the combination of tradition and modernity of the Berber wool carpet, entirely handmade. And you know what? We're sure you'll find the best Moroccan rugs here.

Are you looking for a made-to-measure rug?

If you have very particular dimensions in mind, we are also here for you. To satisfy your wildest desires, we offer to make your carpet to measure, according to the size and finish you want.
